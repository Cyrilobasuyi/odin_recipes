How to make Jollof Rice

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long-grain parboiled rice
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, blended
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Rinse the rice in cold water until the water runs clear, then drain.
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the blended red bell pepper and cook for about 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Stir in the rice, ensuring it is well coated with the pepper mixture.
  5. Add the chicken stock, thyme, salt, and pepper. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat to low and cover.
  6. Cook for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot, garnished with chopped green bell pepper.
